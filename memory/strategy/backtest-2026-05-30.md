# Strategy Backtest — 2026-05-30

## What Was Tested
5 strategy classes against ~10 months of 1d closes for 4 symbols, plus a
4-quarter BTC regime test. Strategies:

| Class | Module |
|---|---|
| `LiquiditySweepStrategy` | `trading/strategies/liquidity_sweep.py` |
| `TrendContinuationStrategy` | `trading/strategies/trend_continuation.py` |
| `BreakoutExpansionStrategy` | `trading/strategies/breakout_expansion.py` |
| `EmaMomentumStrategy` | `trading/strategies/ema_momentum.py` |
| `RSIMACDStrategy` (baseline) | `trading/strategy.py` |

## Headline Result — NO STRATEGY HIT THE 3/4 BAR

| Strategy | +Q | Trades | Win% | Total PnL | Avg Return |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **LiquiditySweep** | **1/4** | 17 | 62.5% | **+$0.777** | +0.20% |
| EmaMomentum | 1/4 | 40 | 43.3% | -$0.699 | -0.18% |
| RSI_MACD_Baseline | 0/4 | 0 | — | $0.000 | 0.00% |
| TrendContinuation | 0/4 | 16 | 46.7% | -$0.358 | -0.09% |
| BreakoutExpansion | 0/4 | 4 | 0.0% | -$0.377 | -0.10% |

`+Q` = positive-return quarters out of 4 BTC segments. Higher = more regime-resilient.

## What This Means
- **LiquiditySweep** is the only strategy with both a positive aggregate PnL and concentrated wins on lower-liquidity symbols (SOL 75% win, XRP 100% win across small samples). It's the most plausible candidate.
- It still failed the regime test (1/4 BTC quarters positive). Reading that result honestly:
  - Each BTC quarter ≈ 75 1d-candles; strategy warmups consume ~40, leaving ~35 effective candles per regime.
  - 35 candles is a tiny sample for a regime-resilience claim. The result is directional, not definitive.
- **RSI+MACD baseline** and **BreakoutExpansion** essentially don't fire on 1d (0 and 4 trades total). They were calibrated for 4h.

## Decision
**Selected approach: stay in DEMO; paper-watch LiquiditySweep for 2 weeks before any wiring decision.**

- Do NOT wire any strategy into `trading/executor.py` this session.
- Do NOT flip `TRADING_MODE` to LIVE.
- Do NOT add capital.
- Continue running ClawBot in DEMO mode (current state).
- Observe LiquiditySweep's HIGH-confidence signals on live data over the next ~14 days.
- Compare live signal cadence and direction-accuracy against the backtest's expected ~17 trades / 62.5% win rate.

## Why Defer Wiring (vs. wire now, paper-trade after)
The workflow's hard rule #4 — "Never wire a strategy into the executor based on 50-day data" — extends naturally to "or based on a 1/4-quarter regime test." Wiring now means we'd be making a code change based on data the regime test explicitly didn't validate.

Paper-watching first means the executor stays in a known-good state, and we get an independent live-data check before committing code.

## Sample Size Caveat (read before re-using these numbers)
- 300 1d candles / 4 symbols = 1,200 candle-observations
- Across 5 strategies = 6,000 strategy-decision evaluations
- But only ~77 actual trades fired across all 5 strategies on all 4 symbols
- The aggregate PnL numbers are sensitive to individual trades. A single outlier on SOL or XRP would shift the ranking.
- This is **directional evidence about which strategy deserves more attention**, not a verdict.
