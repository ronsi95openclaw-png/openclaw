## Recommended campaign structure

- **Objective:** Leads (instant form) OR Messages. For a one-person/pre-launch crew,
  **Messages -> WhatsApp/Messenger** or **Leads (instant form)** convert best and need no
  website. Pick Leads if you want name+phone in a sheet; Messages if you want to chat.
- **Budget:** Start $10-15/day, 1 ad set, learn for 5-7 days before scaling.
- **Location:** DFW metro - target your service radius (e.g. 20-25 mi around your ZIP,
  or specific cities: Dallas, Fort Worth, Arlington, Irving, Plano, Garland, Mesquite).
- **Age/Gender:** 25-65+, all. **Detailed targeting:** homeowners, recently moved,
  "home improvement", "real estate" (realtors/landlords book a lot of cleanouts).
- **Placements:** Advantage+ (automatic) to start.
- **CTA button:** "Get quote" (Leads) or "Send message" (Messages).
